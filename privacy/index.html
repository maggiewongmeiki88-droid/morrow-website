Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Morrow ("we", "our", or "the App") is a personal cost-per-use tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Morrow is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Authentication tokens (provided by Apple Sign In or Google Sign In)

1.2 Data You Create

  • Item names, prices, and purchase dates
  • Usage records (days used or times used)
  • Custom categories
  • Photos you upload for items
  • Wishlist items
  • Notes and descriptions

1.3 Usage Analytics

  • Anonymous usage data (such as feature usage frequency and crash reports)
  • This data cannot be used to identify you personally

2. How We Store Your Data

2.1 Local Storage

  • All your personal data is stored locally on your device by default
  • Data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you enable cloud backup

2.2 Cloud Storage (Pro/Ultra Only)

  • If you subscribe to Pro or Ultra and enable cloud backup, your data is stored securely using Supabase
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • You can disable cloud backup at any time from Settings

3. Third-Party Services

Morrow uses the following third-party services:

3.1 Authentication

  • Apple Sign In: For secure authentication (we only receive your email address)
  • Google Sign In: For secure authentication (we only receive your email address and basic profile)
  • Supabase Authentication: For managing user accounts and sending magic link emails from support@morrow-app.com

3.2 Cloud Services

  • Supabase: For cloud data backup and synchronization (Pro/Ultra only)

3.3 Payment Processing

  • Apple App Store: For processing In-App Purchases and subscriptions
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and analytics

Each third-party service operates under their own privacy policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process your subscription through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Send you account-related emails (magic links, password resets) from support@morrow-app.com
  • Improve the App based on anonymous usage patterns
  • Respond to your support requests sent to info@morrow-app.com

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We will only share information if required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the App
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of analytics collection through the App's settings
  • Request information about what data we hold

6.1 Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by:

  1. Going to Settings → Account Management
  2. Selecting "Delete Account"
  3. Confirming deletion

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted from our servers within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you delete the App or manually delete your data
  • Cloud data: Stored until you delete your account or disable cloud backup
  • Deleted data: Permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

Morrow is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for cloud-stored data
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside of Hong Kong SAR. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Update notice on our website

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

📧 General Inquiries: info@morrow-app.com

📧 Account Support: support@morrow-app.com (for magic link emails)
