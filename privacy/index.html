Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

Morrow ("we", "our", or "the App") is a personal planning and productivity application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Morrow is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Data You Create: All tasks, plans, notes, and personal data you enter in the App are stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Morrow Pro or Ultra, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment details.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data (such as feature usage frequency and crash reports) to improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To process your subscription through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • To improve the App based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage

Your personal data (tasks, plans, notes) is stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud sync (when available), your data is stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

Morrow may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For processing In-App Purchases and subscriptions
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and analytics
  • Apple iCloud: For optional data synchronization

Each third-party service operates under their own privacy policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We will only share information if required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

Morrow is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the App
  • Request information about what data we hold
  • Opt out of analytics collection through the App's settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App or our website.
