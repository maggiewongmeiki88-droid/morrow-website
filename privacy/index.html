Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Welcome to Morrow! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address (for magic link login)
  • Profile Data: Name, profile picture (optional)
  • Journal Entries: Text, photos, voice recordings you create
  • Mood & Wellness Data: Mood ratings, wellness tracking information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Analytics: Crash reports, performance metrics (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve the App's functionality
  • Enable magic link authentication
  • Sync your data across devices (if enabled)
  • Analyze app performance and fix bugs
  • Send important updates about the App
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Local Storage

By default, your journal entries and personal data are stored locally on your device. This data is not accessible to us unless you enable cloud backup.

3.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable cloud backup:

  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored securely on our servers
  • Used only for syncing across your devices
  • You can disable cloud backup anytime in Settings

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics tools (with strict data protection agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: To understand app usage (anonymized data only)
  • Cloud Storage: For optional data backup
  • Authentication: For secure magic link login

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the App
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of analytics collection through the App's settings
  • Request information about what data we hold

6.1 How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data by:

  1. Going to Settings → Account Management
  2. Selecting "Delete Account"
  3. Confirming deletion

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted from our servers within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you delete the App or manually delete your data
  • Cloud data: Stored until you delete your account or disable cloud backup
  • Deleted data: Permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

Morrow is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for cloud-stored data
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside of Hong Kong SAR. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Update notice on our website

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
